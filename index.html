How to play

Fill the grid so every cell shows a sun or a moon.

  • Each row and column ends up with exactly three suns and three moons.
  • The same symbol never sits in three touching cells, across or down.
  • Cells linked by = always hold the same symbol.
  • Cells linked by × always hold opposite symbols.

Tap a cell to place a sun, tap again for a moon, and a third time to clear it. Shaded cells are fixed. Every puzzle has one solution you can reach with logic alone — no guessing needed.
